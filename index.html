About Me

üåª

Hello! I'm Emma Green, an environmental advocate and sustainability blogger based in Portland, Oregon. For the past 8 years, I've been on a mission to live a more eco-friendly lifestyle and inspire others to do the same.

What started as a simple desire to reduce my carbon footprint has evolved into a full-blown passion for environmental conservation. Through this blog, I share practical tips, personal experiences, and scientific insights about sustainable living.

I believe that every small action counts. Whether it's choosing reusable bags, composting food waste, or supporting local farmers, we all have the power to make a positive impact on our planet.

"We don't need a handful of people doing zero waste perfectly. We need millions of people doing it imperfectly." - Anne Marie Bonneau
